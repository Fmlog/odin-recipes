Steak

Description

A steak, also sometimes called "beef steak", is a meat generally sliced across the muscle fibers, potentially including a bone. It is normally grilled, though it can also be pan-fried.

Ingredients

  • 4 (1/2 pound) cube steaks (pounded round meat)
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, briefly brown the cube steaks.
  4. Arrange meat in a single layer in a 13x9 inch baking dish and pour the soup over the top.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour.
